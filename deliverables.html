How We Deliver

Fully managed every step.

Our phishing simulation service team manages every aspect of the campaign from setup and distribution all the way to reporting. After an initial consultation with one of our trained security architects, we ensure correct configuration is in place to execute the campaign. We then design the campaign according to your exact specifications and launch the campaign on an agreed date.

We have loads of phishing campaign templates for you to choose from and customise, including:

  • Specifically used business services example: Salesforce or bespoke software used daily
  • Generally used business services example: Dropbox
  • Personation of internal business leaders Example: HR issue email
  • Targeted attack on C-level staff
  • ‘Wide-net’ phishing email

Practice, Practice, Practice

Research shows that the most effective way to protect end-users from being phished is by continuous use of phishing simulations. After all, practice makes perfect.

Our managed Phishing Simulation Service includes four simulated phishing campaigns a year, and has specific recommendations for users who have fallen victim of the simulated phishing attack. Practice, feedback and training are the three pillars that will reduce susceptible behaviour to phishing attacks by up to 95%.

Within a week of running the simulated phishing attack, the results are compiled into a report. The report also provides an industry benchmark to help you rank your overall exposure to this type of threat. We produce the reports for you to make it easy to see which users have:

  • Received the email
  • Opened the email
  • Clicked on the email link
  • Submitted data on landing page
  • Overall Phish Percentage
  • Industry benchmark comparison

Bespoke campaigns : Blackswan security simulation campaigns can be customised in an infinite number of ways. You can test all your users, or choose specific recipients by department or seniority. You can also customise the action in the phishing email, such as resetting a password, clicking a dodgy link or entering data on a bogus on a landing page. Whatever the requirements, our phishing experts will work with you to provide the exact campaign variables you need.
